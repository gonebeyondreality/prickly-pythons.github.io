Welcome to the SESE Python Users Group

We create this repository as a discussion and sharing platform for Python users and learners at the School of Earth and Space Exploration (SESE), Arizona State University. The repository will allow you to post questions, discuss and share codes with your follow colleagues. Every week, we will pick some codes, questions or topics that are uploaded or requested on the platform and discuss them at brown-bag lunch meeting (Time TBD). You do not have to join us at the lunch meeting to use our platform.

To join the platform, please send your GitHub username to one of our admins below.
